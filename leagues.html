Leagues:

All teams are two archers each.
Choose your own team or we may help you find a team member.
All leagues are 16 teams max.
Make-up days will be planned as needed.
All league fees are non-refundable.
At the end of the league season, we will have a dinner and ceremony for all league participants.

Bronze League

  • 10 Week League – 3/22/2017 to 5/24/2017, 6:30 PM
  • 10 Yards
  • Compound/Recurve (Use of JCA Equipment is permitted)
  • 3 Minutes/End
  • 500-point game (10 ends, 5 arrows each, 50 points max each end)
  • $8/archer/Week
  • Trophy Prize (1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners)

Silver League

  • 12 Week League – 3/22/2017 to 6/7/2017, 7:30 PM
  • 20 Yards
  • Compound/Recurve (Use of JCA Equipment is permitted)
  • 2 Minutes/End
  • 300-point game (10 ends, 3 arrows each, 30 point max each end)
  • $10/archer/Week
  • 15% Grand Prize

Gold League

  • 12 Week League – 3/22/2017 to 6/7/2017, 8:30 PM
  • 20 Yards
  • Compound/Recurve (MUST use own equipment, arrows and points MUST be same diameter)
  • 2 Minutes/End
  • 360-point game (12 ends, 3 arrows each, 30 point max each end)
  • $20/archer/Week
  • 40% Grand Prize
