Hunger Games Anniversary Shoot

March 23rd, 2017 - 7:30 PM
To celebrate the anniversary of The Hunger Games movie release (5 years ago), we will have a very special bracket style shoot-off competition. Every participant is encouraged to try their hardest and move up the ranks. Eventually, there can only be one winner! We will be starting the movie at 8 PM sharp and if you fall out the bracket, you can stay to watch the movie. An amazing prize will be awarded to the winner (prize to be announced before the shoot)!

More
Please note, two of tonight's Archery 101 classes are nearing maximum capacity. Available lane rentals will be very limited or unavailable during those times. Open shoot is available from 10 am to 1 pm and 9 pm to 10 pm. If you are considering a lane rental between 1:30 pm and 9 pm, please call ahead (201-420-9700) to confirm availability.
